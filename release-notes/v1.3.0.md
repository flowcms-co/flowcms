## Highlights

A big one. FlowCMS now manages your subscription end to end from inside the studio, installs like an app on your phone, and keeps your license in sync on its own.

- **Self-serve billing.** A new Subscription and billing section under Settings, Plan shows your plan, renewal date and the card on file. Update your payment method, change plans, cancel or resume, and retry a failed charge, all without leaving the studio.
- **Branded invoices.** Download invoices as clean, FlowCMS-designed PDFs with the service period, payment method and issuer details.
- **Mobile and PWA.** The studio is fully responsive with a real mobile-app feel: a fixed header with a hamburger menu, a bottom tab bar, and Add to Home Screen so you can install it like a native app on iOS and Android.
- **Automatic license sync.** Installs check in with a lightweight twice-daily heartbeat, so paid licenses renew on their own and pause, cancel or plan changes take effect without juggling keys by hand. It is privacy-light (edition, version and aggregate counts only) and can be turned off with `FLOWCMS_TELEMETRY=off`.

## Fixes and improvements

- Billing portal wired to Stripe: update the card with a secure card form, list and download invoices, upgrade or downgrade, cancel or resume, and pay a past-due invoice. It only appears for installs billed through the vendor console.
- The Pro price on the plan page now reflects the live price from the vendor console; Enterprise stays custom.
- Mobile polish: the avatar card no longer crushes its text, dashboard stat cards sit two-up, global search collapses to an icon, and the SEO health ring renders reliably on first paint.
- General stability and performance improvements.
