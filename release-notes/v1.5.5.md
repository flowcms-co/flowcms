# v1.5.5: hand-painted avatar and illustration art

The v2 avatar and dashboard illustration packs, whose per-slot fallback system
shipped earlier, now carry finished art. Soft, hand-painted watercolor
characters in the same storybook style as the guided-tour scene replace the
placeholder and legacy 3D pieces across the app.

- **Character avatars.** A matched set of illustrated people, front-facing on a
  light pastel background so each one crops cleanly to a circle in profile spots,
  the top bar, team lists, comments, and activity feeds. Fourteen slots, thirteen
  filled today.
- **Dashboard hero illustrations.** All fourteen desk scenes, each figure matching
  its avatar, composed as a soft floating vignette on a transparent background so
  they sit naturally on the overview.
- **Refreshed email art.** The six transactional email hero images (welcome, team
  invite, password reset, content published, alert, activity digest) repainted to
  match the pack.
- **Per-slot fallback keeps partial packs safe.** Any slot without v2 art keeps
  showing the classic character with no gaps; avatar slot 6 ships on the classic
  fallback until its art lands.

Studio assets only; no code, no new endpoints, no schema changes.
