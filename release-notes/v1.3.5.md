## Fixes and improvements

- **A gentle nudge as your team grows.** On the free Community edition, once a workspace has
  several members the Team settings page now shows a dismissible suggestion to consider Pro for
  custom roles, field-level permissions, approval workflows and audit logging. Community stays
  uncapped: nothing is blocked, and the hint disappears once you dismiss it (and only resurfaces
  if the team keeps growing).
- **Correct billing console link.** The in-app plan and pricing screens now read live pricing
  from admin.flowcms.co, replacing an old temporary address.
