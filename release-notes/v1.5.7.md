# v1.5.7: compress in-app and email art for fast loads

The guided-tour panels, the v2 avatar and illustration packs, and the
transactional email illustrations shipped as full-size PNGs, roughly 92 MB of
art in total. That made the studio's tour and dashboard slow to paint and
bloated every email send. This release re-encodes and right-sizes all of it
with no visible quality loss at display size.

- **Studio art to WebP.** The tour screenshots, the v2 avatars, and the v2
  illustrations are now WebP, resized to their real display dimensions (avatars
  to 640px, tour and illustrations to 1280px). Browsers all support WebP, so the
  studio loads the same images at a fraction of the bytes.
- **Email art to JPEG.** The six transactional email illustrations move to JPEG
  rather than WebP, because several email clients (notably Outlook on Windows)
  don't render WebP. The images are opaque, so JPEG loses nothing and stays
  universally safe across mail clients.
- **94% smaller.** About 92 MB of image assets drop to roughly 5.7 MB. Faster
  tour and dashboard loads in the studio, and lighter, quicker-loading emails.

No schema changes, no new endpoints, no API changes. Asset and reference update
only: the studio tour and avatar helpers and the mail templates now point at the
new file extensions.
