# v1.6.0: the upgrade dialog shows your annual discount

When you upgrade to Pro on an annual plan there's a standing discount applied
automatically at checkout. Until now the in-app Upgrade dialog showed the full
annual price and then Stripe charged the discounted amount, a nice surprise, but
a confusing one. Now the dialog shows the real price up front.

- **Discounted annual price in the Upgrade dialog.** Settings, Billing, Upgrade
  to Pro now reflects the annual discount: the yearly total shows the discounted
  amount with the list price struck through and the percent off, and the "Save"
  figure is the true saving versus monthly. It reads the live discount, so it
  always matches what Stripe charges at checkout.
- **Monthly is unchanged.** The discount is annual-only; monthly checkout keeps
  the manual promo-code box.

No schema changes and no new endpoints. Studio.
