## v1.4.9: workspace menu layering fix

A small Studio fix.

- **Workspace switcher floats correctly.** On desktop, opening the workspace menu (top of the
  sidebar) could render it behind the page content where the two overlapped. The menu now layers
  above the content and top bar, as a popover should. No layout or behavior change otherwise.

Studio-only, a stacking-order (z-index) fix; no API changes.
