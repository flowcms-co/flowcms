## Highlights

The block editor now folds. Long pages with lots of sections and nested fields stay easy to navigate: every section and repeatable block can collapse, so you can focus on one part at a time instead of scrolling past everything.

## What's new

- Collapse and expand sections: each block in the page builder folds with a click. Collapsed blocks show a short preview of their content, so you can still tell them apart at a glance.
- Collapse and expand nested fields: repeatable components (like a list of services or FAQ items) fold the same way, all the way down through nested levels.
- Expand all / Collapse all: a single toggle folds or opens an entire list at once, handy for pages with many sections.
- Clearer nesting: deeply nested blocks now use alternating background shades so it's obvious what sits inside what.
- Reliable folding: a block stays folded where you left it, even after you reorder, duplicate, or delete other blocks around it.

These changes are purely in the editing experience. Your content, published pages, and APIs are unchanged.
