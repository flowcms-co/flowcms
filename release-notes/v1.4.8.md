## v1.4.8: in-app self-serve Pro checkout

Upgrade to Pro without leaving your install. The Plan & license screen now starts a real checkout,
so there's no need to buy on the website and paste a key back.

- **Buy Pro in-app.** The Pro card's button opens a short dialog to choose seats and monthly or
  annual billing (priced live from your vendor), then sends you to Stripe's secure checkout and
  straight back to your studio. The card stays with Stripe; your install never handles it.
- **Automatic activation.** After payment, the license is issued and bound to this install, and the
  studio pulls it in on the next heartbeat, Pro unlocks on its own, with no key to copy. If the
  payment is still settling, it shows an honest "activating shortly" and finishes by itself.
- **Enterprise stays sales-assisted.** Enterprise is custom-priced, so its card routes to the sales
  team rather than self-serve checkout.
- Managing an existing subscription (seats, plan change, card, cancel) is unchanged; that already
  lived in the Subscription & billing panel.

Requires a vendor billing account with self-serve checkout configured; installs without it keep the
license-key flow. Studio and API changes; existing licenses and the offline-key path are unaffected.
