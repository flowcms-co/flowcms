## Highlights

Seats are now first‑class on Pro.

- **3 seats included, more on demand.** Pro comes with 3 seats; add or remove more yourself from Settings, Plan at $15/mo (or $180/yr) each. Changes are prorated automatically.
- **Seat‑aware invites.** If you're out of seats when inviting a teammate, an owner can add a seat and send the invite in one step, no trip to billing required.

## Fixes and improvements

- The team invite now respects your seat count. At the limit, owners get an inline "add a seat and invite" option; everyone else is pointed to an owner.
- General stability and performance improvements.
