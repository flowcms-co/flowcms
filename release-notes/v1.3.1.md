## Highlights

A quick follow-up to v1.3.0 that makes billing and account emails work the way a self-hosted product should, and adds a place for your company details.

- **Organization and billing details.** A new section under Settings, Plan for your company / legal name, billing address, tax ID and billing email. These show up on your invoices.
- **Manually-licensed plans get a status card.** If your plan was activated with a license key (no card on file, common for Enterprise), you now see your plan and how billing is handled instead of an empty billing area.
- **Account emails point at your own dashboard.** Your install reports its own domain, so any account or billing email links back to your FlowCMS, not a central address.

## Fixes and improvements

- Invoices now show your company name and billing address.
- General stability and performance improvements.
