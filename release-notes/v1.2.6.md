## Highlights

This is a small fix release for the relation picker added in v1.2.5: the list of entries to link now always shows up and is easy to pick from.

## Fixes and improvements

- Relation dropdown no longer hides: when you click a Reference field in the editor, the list of entries to choose from now appears reliably instead of being clipped behind the surrounding panel. It also opens upward automatically when the field sits near the bottom of the page, so the options are never cut off.
- Readable options for reference data without a title: for reference types keyed by something other than a title (for example a Geo Library of cities keyed by name and slug), the picker now lists and searches entries by their slug instead of showing "Untitled".
- General stability and performance improvements.
