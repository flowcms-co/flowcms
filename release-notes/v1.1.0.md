## Highlights

Assisted auto-mapping arrives. Connecting your existing site to FlowCMS is now guided by a visual field mapper: you point at elements on your page instead of writing CSS selectors by hand. White-label theming is also much steadier.

## What's new

- Visual field mapper: click elements on your live site to bind them to content fields, backed by selector maps stored in the CMS.
- Broader importer for faster setup when connecting a site.
- Global search now queries your real content and media.

## Fixes and improvements

- White-label theming loads cleanly with no flash of the default branding, and the app chrome follows your accent color.
- Workspace switcher fits the sidebar; the brand icon toggles the collapsed state.
- The upgrade lock now applies across the whole app.
- Pluralization, notifier targeting, and powered-by wordmark fixes.
